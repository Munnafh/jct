About Us

J C Technology The company is engaged in providing Total IT Solution

and After Sales Support of all types of IT Equipment – Desktop/Laptop/Server, EPBAX – Voice over IP, Network Switches & Routers, Network Security & Firewall, CCTV & Surveillance systems, Printers & Office Automation products and Barcode & Hand held devices.

Our Products used in all verticals – Corporate Offices, Factory sites, Govt. Offices, Govt. & Private Hospitals, Hotels & Restaurants, Schools & Educational institutes and Shopping Malls & Departmental Stores. The Company is located in Burdwan, West Bengal to cater the needs of major mining & Industrial zone of Eastern part of India. It can meet the needs of Bokaro – Dhanbad – Asansol – Durgapur and Kolkata Industrial belt. The company is also equipped to supply service products in Orissa market. The company has team of competent and qualified techno commercial personal to design solution as per the customer’s requirement & expectations. The team is fully committed for after sales support. We have alliance with leading brands of IT Company on the planet -- is of extraordinary depth and width. We have back to back support arrangement with these branded companies. Our association extends to other industry giants HP, Lenevo, DELL, CISCO, D-LINK, KONCA MINOLTA etc. We are committed for best in class products along with best in class support and services. Every member of this team is imbibed with our fundamental ethos- The Customer is the most important entity in the entire ecosystem. We believe in total cost of ownership in entire Product life cycle and take full responsibility of post implementation support.
